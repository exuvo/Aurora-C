After the devastating world war 3 in the early 21th century which killed billions and left the rest starving
or without the amenities they had become used to, humanity fell yet again from the glory it had achieved.
But hard times grow hard people and after many decades humanity recovered and even surpassed its former glory.
The victors renamed the last war and it became known as the great unification wars.
At the dawn of the 22th century with humanity unified under $NATION it is finally ready to continue exploring the stars.
